🖥️ About

The AMICO 2000 (Advanced MIcro COmputer) was an Italian home computer designed by ASEL in Milan and published in the magazine "Sperimentare" starting December 1978. Based on the MOS 6502 processor running at 1MHz, it featured 1KB of RAM, a hexadecimal keypad, and 6 seven-segment LED displays.

This emulator runs the original monitor ROM, allowing you to enter and execute 6502 machine code programs just like on the original hardware.
